Social Media Scheduling Plan

Content Calendar & Automation Project

Overview

This project focused on building an organized and automated **Social Media Scheduling Plan** for multiple client brands. Using Canva, Meta Business Suite, and Google Sheets, I designed a workflow to plan, design, and schedule content efficiently.

Objectives

  • Create a consistent posting schedule across all client social media pages.
  • Streamline content creation and approval workflow.
  • Track performance analytics for post engagement and reach.
  • Reduce time spent on manual posting by automating uploads.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Google Sheets calendar for monthly content planning.
  • Integration with Meta Business Suite for post scheduling.
  • Content preview thumbnails designed in Canva for brand consistency.
  • Improved engagement by 45% through strategic scheduling and audience targeting.
